\subsection{Detailed Description}
A container or holding \hyperlink{classSpecies}{Species} and Reactions. 

The \hyperlink{classCompartment}{Compartment} class is a container for \hyperlink{classSpecies}{Species}, Reactions, and diffusion Reactions that can occur. A \hyperlink{classCompartment}{Compartment} object keeps track of the above while also holding pointers to its neighbors in order to generate diffusion Reactions and other cross-\/compartment Reactions.

\hyperlink{classCompartment}{Compartment} initialization looks like the following\+: 
\begin{DoxyCode}
\hyperlink{classCompartment}{Compartment} *C1 = \textcolor{keyword}{new} \hyperlink{classCompartment_a582e5f8a00d5a30f07f7f653637d7aef}{Compartment}();
\hyperlink{classSpecies}{Species} *A = C1->addSpecies(\textcolor{stringliteral}{"A"},99U);
C1->\hyperlink{classCompartment_ae08ca5d423b24cd7a53fcd88670141a1}{setDiffusionRate}(A,2000);
\end{DoxyCode}
