\subsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a single coordinate between \hyperlink{classCylinder}{Cylinders}, and holds forces needed for mechanical equilibration. 

Beads are the \char`\"{}hinges\char`\"{} between \hyperlink{classCylinder}{Cylinders}. In the minimization algorithms, beads are moved corresponding to external forces, for example, \hyperlink{classFilament}{Filament} stretching and bending. The bead class contains currernt coordinates and forces, and has functions to calculate dot products for the minimization algorithms.
