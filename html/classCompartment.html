Detailed Description

A container or holding Species and Reactions.

The Compartment class is a container for Species, Reactions, and diffusion Reactions that can occur. A Compartment object keeps track of the above while also holding pointers to its neighbors in order to generate diffusion Reactions and other cross-compartment Reactions.

Compartment initialization looks like the following:

Compartment *C1 = new Compartment();
Species *A = C1->addSpecies("A",99U);
C1->setDiffusionRate(A,2000);
