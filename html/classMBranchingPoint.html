Detailed Description

Represents the mechanical component of a BranchingPoint.

The class describes interaction between 4 Beads connected by a BranchingPoint, and its associated equilibrium constants. The branch is a connection between two Cylinders, where one resides on the mother Filament and the other is the actual branch. This is positioned on a Cylinder with the field position (between 0 and 1), held by the parent BranchingPoint.
