Detailed Description

A container to store a MCylinder and CCylinder.

Cylinder class is used to manage and store a MCylinder and CCylinder. Upon intialization, both of these components are created. Extending the Movable and Reactable classes, the Cylinder can update its position and reactions according to mechanical equilibration.
