Detailed Description

For initailizing chemical reactions based on a specific system.

ChemManager is used for initailizing all chemistry in the system. Initialized by the CController, ChemManager initializes the chemical components of the CompartmentGrid. The ChemManager can also update chemical components of a CCylinder using its stored collection of FilamentReactionTemplate, which is initialized at startup.
